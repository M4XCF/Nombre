Welcome to Penguin Statistics!

Penguin Statistics is the Department of Data Analysis of Penguin Logistics. We devote ourselves to item drop analysis. Penguin Statistics' current dataset is maintained through a combination of player manual reports, screenshot recognitions, and other uploads via public APIs. The Penguin Statistics team performs various types of automatic, scheduled, and manual maintenance to ensure the dataset's accuracy.
